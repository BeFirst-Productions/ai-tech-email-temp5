How can AI make every
port operation faster,
smarter, and more
transparent?

Around 80% of transport and logistics companies now prioritize supply chain visibility, and AI is one of the main technologies helping them achieve it.

In modern ports, AI and related digital tools are improving operational transparency and performance through

• Real-time cargo tracking
• IoT-based equipment monitoring
• Remote diagnostics and automated reporting
• Data-driven decision support
• Early identification of bottlenecks and delays

These capabilities help ports reduce turnaround times, improve coordination, increase throughput, and maintain more reliable operations across the supply chain.

AITECH 2026 will highlight how ports and logistics operators are adopting AI to enhance visibility, responsiveness, and efficiency in daily operations.
